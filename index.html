>Projects

  • Pupil Pong >> I helped co-deveop Pupil Pong, a modified version of the game Pong, using eye tracking and voice commands for accessibility. Our project won 2nd place at HackKU23 in the Accessibility theme track. Pupil Pong allows hands-free gameplay through eye tracking and voice recognition, benefiting people with limited physical abilities. Our aim was to broaden its use for various disabilities in the future.
